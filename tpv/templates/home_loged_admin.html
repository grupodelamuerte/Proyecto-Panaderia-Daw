 TPV

Sale zone. Search for tickets and products, categorize products and quickly manage the sale. See the product's description by moving the mouse over it and waiting.

Members

Member zone. Enabled only for the boss. Sign new members in the bakery and edit their profile.

Clients

Clients management zone. Sign new clients and edit ther information. Multiple insertion if needed.

Products

Products management zone. Create, edit and delete all the stock. Multiple insertion if needed. Delete within the countdown to speed up the process.

Tickets

Tickets management zone. Show or delete ticket's details. Clean empty tickets from unfinished sales. Sort rows by field. Search by text or date.
